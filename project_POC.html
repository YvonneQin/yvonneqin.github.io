Spinning the wheel of product growth by Design & Insight - Sneak Peak to Narratives POC demo Brief intro

In clinical trials, "Patient Safety Narratives" are comprehensive and structured descriptions of adverse events and safety-related information pertaining to participants in the study. These narratives play a crucial role in conveying the context, details, and clinical significance of adverse events or safety findings to regulatory authorities, ethics committees, and other stakeholders involved in the trial.

POC Rapid Prototyping

This demo video was displayed at US SAPA exhibition to support customer approach.

Spinning the wheel of product growth by Design & Insight

I found that, based on the clinical trial document workflow, Patient Safety Narratives would serve as a very good medium to bridge the design of two product lines: Create (Medical Writing Solutions) and Compute (Statistics Solutions), thus becoming a wheel for driving product growth.
